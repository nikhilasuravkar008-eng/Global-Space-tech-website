Founder CEO

KRISHNA-SINGH

Rajesh spent over 20 years leading pharma commercial operations across India before founding GlobalSpace Technologies. Frustrated by the disconnected, outdated tools pharma field teams were forced to work with, he set out to build a genuinely unified, AI-native platform — one built by people who actually understand the day-to-day realities of the industry, not generic enterprise software adapted after the fact.

Under his leadership, GlobalSpace has grown from a single-product idea into a connected ecosystem spanning commercial excellence, distribution, and healthcare delivery.
